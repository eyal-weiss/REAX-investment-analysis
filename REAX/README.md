# The Real Brokerage (NASDAQ: REAX) — Investment Analysis

Independent investment analysis of The Real Brokerage Inc., a technology-first residential real estate brokerage that has grown from $122M in revenue (2021) to nearly $2.0B (2025) by recruiting agents onto a cloud-based platform with higher commission splits, equity participation, and integrated financial services.

## Thesis Summary

**Date:** March 2026 | **Data basis:** FY2025 audited financials

REAX operates as the brokerage-of-record for independent real estate agents across North America, earning a ~2.6% take rate on $75.3B in gross merchandise value. The company is building a high-margin financial services layer (title, mortgage, insurance) on top of its agent network.

### Key Investment Pillars

1. **Agent recruitment** into a platform with structural advantages (higher splits, equity, integrated tools)
2. **Financial services build-out** (title, mortgage, insurance) that reshapes the margin profile
3. **Debt-free balance sheet** generating substantial free cash flow funding buybacks and organic investment

## Methodology

- 5-year DCF with Gordon Growth terminal value (WACC 12.0%, terminal growth 3.0%)
- Bottom-up operating model anchored in agent-count growth, per-agent productivity, and per-segment financial services
- Cross-checked with EV/EBITDA and P/E exit multiple valuations
- Scenario analysis across bull, base, and bear cases
